# Doctor Manual

## Purpose

Review patients, document consultations, order investigations, manage admissions, review results, and complete clinical plans.

## OPD consultation

1. Open **Doctor Queue**.
2. Select a patient.
3. Review the patient safety banner.
4. Enter SOAP note:
   - subjective
   - objective
   - assessment
   - plan
5. Add diagnosis.
6. Complete encounter when done.

## Results review

1. Open **Results Inbox**.
2. Review verified lab results and radiology reports.
3. Click **Mark reviewed** after clinical review.

## Referrals

1. Open **Referrals**.
2. Search/select patient.
3. Choose internal or external referral.
4. Enter reason and referral letter.
5. Track referral status.

## Inpatient

1. Open **Admissions**.
2. Select admission.
3. Add daily progress notes.
4. Create discharge summary.
5. Complete discharge summary.
6. Discharge admission when clinically approved.

## Safety notes

- Always check allergies and chronic conditions.
- Lab results must be verified before clinical review.
- Clinical notes should be treated as immutable.
